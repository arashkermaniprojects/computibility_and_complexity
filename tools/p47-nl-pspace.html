NL ⊊ PSPACE · proven strict

Via the space hierarchy theorem.

CLO-5R3·40 ptsL16
1 · Theory
Proof

Savitch: NL ⊆ SPACE(log²n). Space hierarchy: SPACE(log²n) ⊊ SPACE(log² n · log log²n) ⊊ … ⊊ SPACE(n) ⊊ … ⊊ PSPACE. So strict containment NL ⊊ PSPACE.

3 · Q&A defence notes
  • Q1. Why Savitch?
    NL = NSPACE(log n) ⊆ SPACE(log² n) by Savitch. Without it, we'd need a different bound to get inside PSPACE.
  • Q2. Why is the chain "strict everywhere"?
    Each step is a space-hierarchy inclusion: a slightly larger space bound strictly contains the smaller one.
  • Q3. Why don't we know if NL ⊊ P or P ⊊ NP?
    Those compare differently-resourced classes (nondet space vs det time vs nondet time). Hierarchy theorems compare same-resource bounds.
