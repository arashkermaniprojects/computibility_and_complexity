Most computer scientists lean towards the belief that these two classes are distinct, but the lack of a proof is pretty striking. On the flip side, we do have some proven separations, like P being strictly contained in EXPTIME. That’s a clear distinction. It shows just how complex and subtle these relationships can be, making it an intriguing area of study.
