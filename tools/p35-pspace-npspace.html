Theorem (Savitch)

For f(n) ≥ log n: NSPACE(f) ⊆ SPACE(f²). Hence PSPACE = NPSPACE.

Strategy: nondeterministic acceptance ⇔ reachability in the configuration graph. CANYIELD recursively halves the time budget.

3 · Q&A defence notes
  • Q1. Why f² and not just f?
    Recursion depth = log t = O(f) levels. Each frame stores O(f) bits. Total = f · f = f².
  • Q2. Why doesn't this work in TIME?
    Time can't be reused. In space we reuse cells; in time we'd have to actually execute both halves of CANYIELD, summing time.
  • Q3. So NTIME(f) ⊆ TIME(f²)?
    No — that's an open question (related to P vs NP). The proof technique fundamentally relies on space reusability.
